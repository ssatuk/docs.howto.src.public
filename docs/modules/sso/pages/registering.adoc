== Registering an Account

IMPORTANT: This section describes the normal method to create a username and password.
If you are planning to use your Google account to authenticate to our services do not follow these instructions, instead see xref:authenticating-with-google.adoc[this page].

include::partial$partial-use-correct-email.adoc[]


. Go to the https://identity.ssatuk.co.uk/UserAccount/Register[Register your Details^, role="ext-link"]  page
. Enter the username and password you would like to use plus your email (as per the guidance above)  and select btn:[Register]. 
+
NOTE: Your username cannot contain spaces or the `@` symbol. It is unique to SSAT systems and is not linked to your school systems in any way.
+
image::sso-register.png[]

. The next stage will take up to a minute while records are created for you.

== Confirming your email

. Please check your inbox for the verification email from identity@ssatuk.co.uk (check your spam folder as it might be in there) and click the link - on the page it takes you to enter your password to verify your email address. Please note your login will not work until your email address has veen verified.

+
[TIP]
====
If the verification email hasn't arrived within 15 minutes and isn't 
in your junk mail folder, please contact us at help@ssatuk.co.uk for assistance
====
. See the  xref:authenticate-with-username.adoc[login] page for detailed instructions for each service.
. On your very first login you will be asked to accept the SSAT Terms and Conditions - once you have accepted those you will be directed to the service you requested.
